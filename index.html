Biography

I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering in the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, under the supervision of Prof. Bert Shi. My current research interests focus on computer vision, machine learning and robotics. Previously, I receive my M.Phil. in Computer Science in Nanjing University, China. At that moment, my supervisor was Prof. Tong Lu, and my research mainly focused on text detection and recognition in images and videos.
